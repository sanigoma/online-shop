About Caphaus

Founded in 2016, Caphaus brings creativity and self-expression to the streets through unique, high-quality custom streetwear caps.

Our Story

What began as a passion for individuality has grown into a bold streetwear brand recognized for redefining modern cap design. At Caphaus, we believe that what you wear should reflect who you are — authentic, confident, and original.


Every piece is crafted with attention to detail, comfort, and quality, blending urban culture with personal expression. From custom orders to exclusive drops, we exist for those who dare to stand out.

Our Mission

To empower self-expression through bold, high-quality streetwear that speaks your identity. We’re not just selling caps — we’re building confidence, culture, and community.
